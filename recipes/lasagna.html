Lasagna

Ingredients

    2 tbs olive oil
    750g lean beef mince
    90g pack prosciutto
    800g tomato sauce
    200ml hot beef stock
    a little grated nutmeg
    300g fresh lasagna sheets
    white sauce
    125g thin strips of ball mozarella

Steps

    Heat the oil in a skillet and cook the beef in 2 batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
    Finely chop 4 slices of proscuitto and stir into beef mixture.
    Pour half of the tomato sauce and 200ml of hot beef stock. Add nutmeg, then season.
    Bring sauce up to boil and simmer for 30 min.
    Heat oven to 180C and lightly oil an ovenproof dish.
    Spoon 1/3 of the meat sauce into the dish. Cover with some lasagna sheets. Drizzle some white sauce.
    Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta sheets. Cover with remaining white sauce.
    Scatter mozarella on top.
    Arrange the rest of the proscuitto on top. Bake for 45min until top is bubbling and lightly browned.
